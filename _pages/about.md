Aloha! My name is Jensen. I will be joining the junior research staff of the Economic Policy Studies division at the [American Enterprise Institute (AEI)](https://www.aei.org/) as a full-time research assistant in July 2024, with the intent of pursuing graduate studies in economics in the near future. Currently, I am a research assistant at the [University of Hawaii Economic Research Organization (UHERO)](https://uhero.hawaii.edu/) and am in my final semester as an undergraduate at the [University of Hawaii at Manoa](https://manoa.hawaii.edu/), pursuing my BA in the Quantitative Economics Concentration in the College of Social Sciences. I am also affiliated with the Grassroot Institute of Hawaii (GRIH), and am a former research fellow at the Center for Entrepreneurship & Economic Education at Hawaii Pacific University.
