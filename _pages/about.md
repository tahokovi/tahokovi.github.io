Aloha! My name is Jensen Ahokovi. I will be joining the junior research staff of the Economic Policy Studies division at the [American Enterprise Institute (AEI)](https://www.aei.org/economics/) as a full-time research assistant in July 2024 with the intent of pursuing graduate studies in economics in the near future. Currently, I am a research assistant at the University of Hawaii Economic Research Organization (UHERO) and am in my final semester as an undergraduate at the [University of Hawaii at Manoa](https://manoa.hawaii.edu/) pursuing my BA in the Quantitative Economics Concentration in the College of Social Sciences. I am also affiliated as a research associate at the Grassroot Institute of Hawaii (GRIH) and am a former research fellow at the Center for Entrepreneurship & Economic Education at Hawaii Pacific University.
