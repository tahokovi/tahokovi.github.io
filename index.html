About

Welcome! I am a full-time Research Assistant in the Economic Policy Studies Department at the American Enterprise Institute for Public Policy Research (AEI).

My research focuses on the intersection of labor, urban, and public finance. I also have tertiary interests in the intersection of causal inference and machine learning methods. I received my BA in the Quantitative Economics Concentration from the University of Hawaii at Manoa in the summer of 2024.

I intend to pursue a PhD in economics and/or public policy in the near future.
